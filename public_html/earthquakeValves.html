Earthquake Valves

  2. Earthquake Valves

Earthquakes can cause gas piping to crack or rupture, or cause shifting of appliances, severing their fuel lines and creating serious risks. With a properly installed valve on your gas meter, your home or business is protected from earthquake related gas fires 24/7, whether you are there or not.

We exclusively use The Little Firefighter™ earthquake valves to bring you peace of mind. The Little Firefighter™ gas shutoff valve is designed to cut the flow of gas to your building during a significant earthquake. All valves are made in the U.S.A. and thouroughly tested to government and individual standards.

Benefits of The Little Firefighter™ valves include:

  • 5.4 magnitude seismic events triggered automatic gas shut-off
  • Compliant with all California requirements
  • Most compact, lightweight and state-of-the-art design
  • Best flow on the market
  • 30 Year Warranty on all parts
  • Sight glass to easily check valve status
  • Manual reset available
